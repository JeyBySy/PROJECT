BEYLANDS Sales and Services Specialist is a company of sole proprietorship, owned and managed by Ms. Luciana P. Delos Santos in 2011. “BEYLANDS” stands for the first letter of the family’s First Name and Surname which is Bea, Erin, Ycel, Luciana, Ariane, Norberto Delos Santos. The business started as a supplier of janitorial supplies in the whole province of Batangas. Beylands Sales and Service Specialist is primarily engaged in the business of direct selling activities of various janitorial and housekeeping supplies with high quality but affordable cost. Such products are manufactured by well-known manufacturing companies local and international guaranteed to be effective in line of janitorial and housekeeping processes for residential, commercial and industrial establishments and other institutional concern. The Company implements policies and directions promulgated by the owner, who has distinguished herself in the respective lines of endeavor known for her integrity and fair dealing. Beylands Sales and Service Specialist prides itself with high quality, economical products and competitive pricing.
